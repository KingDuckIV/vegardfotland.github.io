Biography

I am a 28 year old musician and artist from the southwest of Norway. I study Digital Culture at the University of Bergen.

Former Bassist in MovePrimer and OCM. Current demiurge behind Color for a Blind Man, The Wayfarer Project and one half of Rathaus.
