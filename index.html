Biography

About

I am a musician, poet and artist from the southwest of Norway. I study Digital Culture at the University of Bergen.

Former Bassist in MovePrimer and OCM. Current demiurge behind Color for a Blind Man, Cull the Living Flower, The Wayfarer Project and one half of Rathaus.

As i am on my last year on the masters program, I spend most of my time writing, or thats at least what i tell my supervisor. Don't tell her please
