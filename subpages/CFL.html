Cold Fluorescent Lights

A post war industrial fantasy campaign about forbidden Pneuma, dream logic, and workers caught between the state and the underworld.

The Sootworks churns. Factories hum. The Pneumoclasts police the ruins of the old war while dream sects whisper about the sea behind the arcane gate. Our party has crossed paths with assassins, union busters, and the Malferent Soulforge, uncovering hints that the gods are not the only storykeepers in town. This page collects a primer for new players and a reference hub for the table.
