NMWP Main Prize thoughts

10 Jan 2025 process gratitude

Ahhh, the new media writing prize, the highest of praise, nominated for both the main and student prize. What a time to be alive ey. Thank you to the judges for dragging me into this thing. I am honored. Eternally so.

Some nights I map small sparks that led here. A page that glitched just right. A teacher who said keep going. A friends pat on the back. It is enough.

Starting my Masters Course

17 Jul 2024 study path

For a long time I stood at a crossroads, roads in mist, each with a rumor. My mind ran. Where am I going. What will it bring. Does it matter. Should I care.

It is only lately I found peace with not knowing. I look at all the roads and I do not mind which one. If I keep making the things I love, if I care for the people near me, then any road becomes mine. Digital Culture felt like a current. I stepped in. It felt right. I am excited to work in this space. To learn. To build. To listen. Whatever the road brings. It will be interesting.

New Media Writing Prize

02 Dec 2024 award reflection

What Remains? was my first digital artwork and my bachelors project. To see it shortlisted felt unreal and distant. I am grateful to the judges, but the deepest joy was my teachers beaming. Alinta Krauth who guided me. Jason Nelson who nudged strange doors open.

It is like music. Applause is warm, but when a musician you respect says they felt it, that is a special fizz. Thank you to the New Media Writing Prize. Huzzah.
